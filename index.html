Our Story

Built by listeners,
for listeners.

Glomi was born from a simple frustration — music streaming had become transactional, algorithmic, hollow. We believed music deserved better.

We built Glomi around the idea that great audio is an emotional experience. Every feature, every design decision, every partnership is in service of that one truth: music moves people.

  • Lossless FLAC & MQA audio formats
  • Zero advertising, ever
  • Artists earn 3× the industry average
  • Privacy-first, no data sold
  • Available on iOS, Android & Web
